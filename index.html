ABOUT ME

My name is Drew Michael and I am 27 years old. I currently work as a UX Designer and also as a working Artist. I previously graduated from Sinclair back in 2012 earning my AAS in Visual Communications. I then transferred to Ohio University and earn my BFA in Studio Art. I have returned to Sinclair to further my education. This time I am pursuing degrees in both Web Development and Software Development.
